NATE TRAINOR

ABOUT


My name is Nate Trainor, a Colorado native and aspiring full-stack developer. A graduate in history from Hillsdale College, I developed a diligent work ethic and fostered a love of learning during my research there. I also learned how to coordinate groups and organize events through some of my leadership positions with on-campus clubs. In my free time, I enjoy learning new languages, writing short stories, modding video games, and using video software.


Following my graduation from Hillsdale, I became interested in computer programming following my interest in program modification and learning new languages. I received a full-stack education at Skill Distillery, where I learned Java, SQL, HTML, CSS, JPQL, and other programming languages. Despite the program's rigor, my established work ethic enabled me to fully immerse myself into Java to pass Oracle's Java SE 8 Programmer I (1Z0-808) exam. I look forward to working with you, and feel free to look at my previous proejcts below or contact me. Thank you for visiting!
